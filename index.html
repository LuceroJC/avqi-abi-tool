🎯 What are AVQI and ABI?

AVQI (Acoustic Voice Quality Index) combines 6 acoustic parameters into a single validated score for overall dysphonia severity. More reliable than single-parameter measurements.

ABI (Acoustic Breathiness Index) combines 9 acoustic parameters specifically designed to quantify breathiness perception with high accuracy.
